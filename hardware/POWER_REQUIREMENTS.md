# Power Requirements

## STM32 NUCLEO-G071RB

- USB Supply: 5V
- Operating Voltage: 3.3V

---

## NXP i.MX 8M Plus EVK

- USB Type-C Supply
- Recommended Adapter: 5V

---

## Sensors

### Potentiometers

- 3.3V

### DS18B20

- 3.3V

---

## Total Hardware

| Device | Supply |
|----------|--------|
| STM32 | USB |
| NXP | USB Type-C |
| Potentiometers | 3.3V |
| DS18B20 | 3.3V |

Ensure a common ground between the STM32 and NXP boards for reliable UART communication.
